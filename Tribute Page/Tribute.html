Some of His Famous Quotes:

  • "Imagination is more important than knowledge."

Interesting Facts About Einstein:

  • He was offered the presidency of Israel in 1952, but declined.
  • He was a pacifist and campaigned for disarmament and nuclear non-proliferation.
  • He was an accomplished violinist and often played music with his friends.
